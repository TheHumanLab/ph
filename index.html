ABOUT PUMA HARVEST

Agriculture with Local Impact

Puma Harvest is a Tanzanian agricultural enterprise focused on poultry production and the development of integrated agricultural value chains.

Our business combines commercial agriculture with community participation, creating opportunities for local employment, skills development, and economic activity. Through trusted local partnerships and disciplined agricultural management, we produce quality poultry products while contributing to the long-term growth of the communities in which we operate.

We believe successful agriculture creates value for customers, partners, and communities alike.

WHAT WE DO

Poultry Production

Producing quality chickens to meet market demand for freshness, consistency, and reliability.

Wholesale Supply

Supplying poultry products to retailers, distributors, restaurants, institutions, and commercial buyers.

Agricultural Value Chain Development

Building stronger connections between production, processing, logistics, and market access.

Supply Chain Coordination

Managing efficient movement of products from production to customer through structured operational systems.

Strategic Agricultural Partnerships

Working with local institutions, producers, and stakeholders to strengthen agricultural participation and long-term sustainability.

Market Expansion

Developing opportunities to expand agricultural products into regional and export markets.
